About the Department

The Department has a long tradition of excellence in educating, mentoring, and inspiring future technology leaders and researchers in the area of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The department offers undergraduate and post graduate programs and a recognized Research centre under the Quality Improvement Program (QIP) of the Government of India where the students are involved in Industry collaborative Research, Consultancy and State of Art Labs which contributes to the social betterment.

Faculty who are very passionate about teaching are also involved in a number of research projects independently and in collaboration with industry and other renowned Research organisations.
